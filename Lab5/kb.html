Yummy Korean BBQ

I learned to love this place while living in Honolulu. I was a commuter, with my family staying at our home in Plano. So had most evenings to myself. I’d walk home from the office, and stop in the Ala Moana shopping center for a barbeque fix.

Gogiya

This is one of my “go to places with friends”. Food is good and plentiful. Biggest decision is which type of table - barbecue or broth. I always vote for barbecue, but sometimes the group decision is broth. (The tables are setup for one or the other but not both.)

Food Court at Legacy

This is a wonderful place to visit. Not one restaurant, but a collection of vendors. Some serve great Korean barbecue. My friends will almost always spend some time investigation all the vendors before making a decision to order. There are so many choices!

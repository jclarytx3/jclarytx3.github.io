Introduction

I enjoy many food types. Two of my favorites are Korean barbeque and Seafood. I also enjoy salads, and Texas barbeque.

Please visit my Korean Barbeque page and my Seafood pagefor more details and my favorite restaurants.

Why Korean Barbeque?

Now it may appear “strange” that a Texan would love Korean Barbeque. One again – the answer is simple. I developed a love for seafood while in Hawaii. I spent over 25 years traveling to Hawaii every other week to visit a major client. I learned to love the food at the take-out place at the Ala Moana mall. I’d stop by there often to get my barbeque fix.

Why Seafood?

A reasonable question might be “why is seafood one of his favorite foods?” Well – the answer is simple. I developed a love for seafood as a kid living on Key Biscayne (part of Miami). The only “real” non-hotel restaurant at that time was a seafood place with a pirate theme. S, it was either learn to appreciate seafood or go hungry.
